About Us

Who We Are

Air Force School Madh Island was established on 02 Jul 84 for providing educational facilities to the children of Air Force Personnel posted to this station. The Air Force school runs under the aegis of the Indian Air Force Educational and Cultural Society (Registration No. PPS/37/SWAC/2003). Air Force School Madh Island is a Pre-Primary School and the strength of students for the AY 2025-26 is 65.
